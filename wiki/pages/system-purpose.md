## System Purpose

Conversations are the source of truth for intent. Everything else is derived.

The system exists because intent is continuously generated through conversation but never persists — it evaporates between sessions. The goal is to close that loop: capture the raw signal, distil it into structured intent, and maintain a living record that future agents can reason against.
