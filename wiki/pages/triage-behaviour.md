## External Content Following

When external links are mentioned in conversations, the triage agent should follow them and consider them for relevancy. Blog posts and other linked content can be directly relevant to the project's intent and should not be discarded just because they are external. [[source:811302f6-0be7-435c-b844-910cc9a21b67/31]]
