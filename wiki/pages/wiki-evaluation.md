## Reference Wiki and Evaluation Framework

A reference wiki should be written manually as ground truth, then different generation strategies evaluated against it. The evaluation needs multiple checks: is the right content included, are the invariants present, are the pages structured sensibly, are they in the right categories. The reference wiki already exists in git history from the initial manual creation. This approach applies the characterisation test pattern to wiki generation itself.
